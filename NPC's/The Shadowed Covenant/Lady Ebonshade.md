**Classic Dark Mistress:**

- **Physical Appearance:** Pale skin, dark, piercing eyes (perhaps black or violet), and long, flowing black hair. Her attire is typically dark, often adorned with symbols of shadow and night. She might wear a long, flowing black cloak that hides her form and casts an air of mystery.
- **Aura:** An aura of dark energy might surround her, subtly shifting shadows and creating an eerie atmosphere.

**The Unassuming Scholar:**

- **Physical Appearance:** She might appear as an ordinary, perhaps slightly gaunt woman, dressed in simple, dark robes. Her appearance could be unremarkable, allowing her to blend in with any crowd.
- **Hidden Depths:** Beneath her unassuming exterior, she possesses a keen intellect and a deep understanding of dark magic. Her eyes might occasionally flash with a sinister light, revealing her true nature to those who know where to look.

**The Fae-like Enchantress:**

- **Physical Appearance:** She could have an ethereal beauty, with pale skin, flowing hair, and eyes that shift between colors. She might wear flowing, otherworldly garments adorned with symbols of nature and magic.
- **Enigmatic Presence:** Her appearance could be subtly shifting and illusory, making it difficult to truly perceive her form. She might use illusions to mask her true intentions and deceive those who underestimate her.
