**Assistant Headmaster of The [[Black Harbor|Black Harbor]] Academy**
 
Spiv Fastboot was born into a humble halfling family in the outskirts of [[Black Harbor|Black Harbor]], a bustling port city in the heart of a predominantly human kingdom. From a young age, Spiv displayed a natural curiosity and a mischievous spirit, often finding himself in the middle of playful adventures with the other halfling children.
 
One fateful day, while wandering through the city's crowded market, Spiv stumbled upon the impressive facade of the Order of the Two Moon, a prestigious mage's guild that stood as a bastion of magical knowledge in the city. Fascinated by the arcane wonders within, he began to spend every free moment observing the mages and wizards as they practiced their craft. The grand hall, filled with levitating books and shimmering lights, sparked a flame of aspiration within Spiv's heart.
 
Despite the skepticism and doubt from his family and some of the guild members, Spiv's determination to prove his magical potential knew no bounds. He set out to convince the Guildmaster Sorin Lockreach, to give him a chance. With relentless tenacity and a handful of small magical tricks up his sleeve, Spiv finally managed to impress Archibald and secured an apprenticeship at the Order of the Two Moon.
