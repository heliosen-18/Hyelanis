**Background:** Talthar is an ancient wizard who has lived for centuries in seclusion atop the jagged peaks of the Midnight Edge mountain range. Once a renowned scholar and practitioner of the arcane arts, he withdrew from society long ago, seeking solitude and enlightenment in the isolation of the mountains.  
**Location:** Talthar's secluded abode is hidden deep within the craggy cliffs of the Midnight Edge, far from the prying eyes of civilization. It takes a full day's journey through treacherous terrain to reach his remote sanctuary from the small town of [[Windmere]].  
**Personality:** Talthar is a mysterious and enigmatic figure, steeped in arcane knowledge and ancient wisdom. He is introverted and introspective, preferring the company of his books and magical experiments to that of other people. Despite his solitary nature, he is not unkind and may extend his wisdom to those who seek it with sincerity.
