## **The Diarchs**  
- King Grimbeard Stonebrow (Dwarf): The current dwarven monarch, known for his gruff exterior and fair-minded rule. He is a master smith and a staunch traditionalist.  
- Queen Feya Glimmerwing (Gnome): The current gnomish monarch, known for her eccentric personality and love of innovation. She is a skilled airship pilot and a powerful magic user.
 
## **The Council**  
- Head Councilor Borin Stonecutter (Dwarf): A respected elder and advisor to King Stonebrow, known for his wisdom and knowledge of dwarven history.  
- Councilor [[Willow Whisperwind]] (Gnome): A representative of Queen Glimmerwing, known for her diplomatic skills and ability to bridge cultural divides.
 
## **Merchants**  
- Gildar Goldpocket (Dwarf): A wealthy merchant who owns a chain of jewelry shops across the empire. He is known for his shrewd business sense and his love of fine gems.  
- Tilly Tinkerton (Gnome): An eccentric inventor and merchant who sells her unique creations in her shop on The Merchant's Mile. She is always experimenting with new and sometimes unpredictable magical devices.
 
## **Artisans**  
- Bardin Stonehammer (Dwarf): A master smith who forges legendary weapons in The Forges of An'dul Khaz'al. He is a gruff but fair teacher, passing on his skills to a select few apprentices.  
- Nimblefinger Geargrinder (Gnome): A renowned clockwork engineer who creates intricate automatons and other wondrous devices. He is always eager to share his knowledge and passion for invention.
 
## **Explorers**  
- Sky Captain Wren Whistlewind (Gnome): A daring airship captain who leads expeditions to uncharted territories. She is known for her navigational skills and her ability to chart even the most treacherous landscapes.
 
## **Other Notable Figures**  
- The Oracle of Keranos (Gnome): A mysterious gnome who resides in a hidden shrine dedicated to Keranos. She is said to have prophetic visions and offer cryptic advice to those who seek her wisdom.  
- The Ancient Smith (Dwarf): A legendary figure who is said to have forged the first dwarven weapons. His spirit is believed to reside in The Forges of An'dul Khaz'al, guiding the hands of master smiths.
