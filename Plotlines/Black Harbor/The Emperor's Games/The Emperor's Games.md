    - ==Alchemy and potion-making take center stage in this contest.     - ==In this lighthearted event, participants compete to see who can handle the most magical concoctions without succumbing to their effects. - ==Event 10: The Deathmatch; The Underking Erebos; The High Lord, The Emperor==
    - ==The most prestigious event of The Emperor Games, the Deathmatch , gathers the most powerful spellcasters and warriors from across the realms. The last team standing claim the title of the Champions of the Realm and receive grand rewards and recognition from the Emperor.==
