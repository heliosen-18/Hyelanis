**Cost:** 58 GP per ton  
**Weight:** 500 pounds per barrel  
**Description:**  
Powdered Sordalite Fuel is a magically infused substance used to power the Shard Furnaces of skyships. This blend of unrefined Sordalite, elemental ash, sulfur, and powdered iron creates a potent energy source that generates the magical vapors necessary for flight. One ton of this fuel can propel a shard blimp for approximately 500 miles at 50% cargo capacity.![[Powdered Sordalite Fuel.jpg]]
