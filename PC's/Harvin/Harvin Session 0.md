[[The Gaunt Man]] sent you information via raven animal messenger to meet with a human by the name of Farling as he knows of the location to the Tomb of the Reborn, which is said to contain the key to finding the [[Book of the Dead]]. With him and his associate, an expert dungeoneer goblin by the name of Thrask Gul set out to Black Peak Mountain, the volcano near [[Black Harbor|Black Harbor]] that gives the city it's name.
