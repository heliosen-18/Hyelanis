## Notable Locations
### **Key Establishments**

- **The Fog and Frog:** This popular tavern is housed in a large converted warehouse. It features a dozen modest rooms for rent in the back and is run by Skeldruff Plenk, who brews a famous lager called "Plenk's Plenk".
- **Uskarn Port:** The village features a port that is unusually large for a settlement of its size. It serves fishing boats, trading vessels, and naval ships, but is also the site of a smuggling operation run by Griff Talsin.


### **Council Member Businesses**

The module identifies the shops owned by the village council members, which serve as the primary commercial locations in Key Landing:

- **Vortanim’s Bookshop:** Run by the elderly druid Vortanim, this location serves as a bookseller's shop and a place for sage advice.
- **Golpin’s General Store:** A general goods store owned by the unscrupulous Marius Golpin.
- **Bellweather’s Cheese Shop:** A shop owned by the halfling Bellis Bellweather.
- **Delvane’s Smithy:** A blacksmith shop run by the retired soldier Mirria Delvane.
- **Arrowen’s Fur and Leather Shop:** A shop run by the elf guide Valissia Arrowen.

### **Transportation**

- **The Ferry:** A small keelboat docks at the village to provide passage across the three miles of water to Firewatch Island.

## NPCs

### **Village of Uskarn (The Council & Residents)**

Key Landing is the starting settlement. The village is run by a council of six merchants, though real power lies with the bailiff.
#### Council Members
- **Vortanim:** (NG male human druid). An elderly sage and bookseller. He is the council member most likely to ask the characters to investigate the hermitage.
- **Skeldruff Plenk:** (LN male gnome commoner). The proprietor of the "Fog and Frog" tavern and a beer maker.
- **Bellis Bellweather:** (CG female halfling commoner). Owner of a cheese shop.
- **Mirria Delvane:** (N female human guard). A retired soldier turned blacksmith.
- **Valissia Arrowen:** (CN female elf spy). A guide and scout who runs a fur and leather shop.
- **Marius Golpin:** (NE male human thug). The unscrupulous owner of a general store.
#### Other NPCs
- **Feldrin Kane:** (LN male human knight). The intimidating bailiff who holds the true power in Uskarn.
- **Merrick:** (N male halfling veteran). Kane’s taciturn agent who commands the deputies.
- **Ulder Raserhill:** (LN male half-orc commoner). The short-tempered ferry master who transports people to Firewatch Island.
- **Griff Talsin:** (CE male human bandit captain). A smuggler and privateer posing as a merchant; he has deputies in his pocket.

### **The Survivors (Firewatch Island)**

These are the primary NPCs the players will interact with inside the hermitage bolt-hole (Area 25).

- **Janore Stormswake:** A human female cleric of Procan. She is the most capable survivor and the one who received the premonition of danger.
- **Barret Gloffrin:** A male human commoner. He is very ill, having contracted "bluerot" from the undead. He will die without magical healing.
- **Morley Tobe:** A male dwarf commoner. He is slightly ill with "bluerot." He is also a fugitive debtor hiding from Davus Raal (see Adventure Hooks).
- **Virgil:** An ancient seagull with an Intelligence of 9. He was the familiar of the fortress's original wizard, Archais. He can communicate via _speak with animals_ and knows the location of the wreck.

### **The Villains & Monsters**

- **Syrgaul Tammeraut:** The "Drowned Master." Formerly a pirate captain and sorcerer, now the undead leader trapped in the Pit of Hatred.
- **Rasp:** A monstrous Peryton. It hunts the coast and is currently using the hermitage belfry as a lair.
- **Harpy Matriarch:** Disguises herself as a sobbing woman on the road to Uskarn to ambush travelers.

### **Deceased / Historical Figures**

These characters are dead upon the party's arrival but are relevant to the lore or investigation.

- **Philpert:** An older human hermit. He escaped the island but died of exhaustion washing up on the beach near Uskarn (the party finds his body first).
- **Aaron Kelderman:** A hermit who survived the initial attack but was killed by Rasp in the Belfry (Area 24) while trying to ring the bell for help.
- **Tallos:** A druid hermit. He died in his cot but left behind assassin vines and potions.
- **Borisanth:** The Old Prior of the hermitage. He took a vow of silence and was killed opening his door.
- **Archais:** The wizard of the original garrison (10 years prior). He died in the original attack; his journal provides the location of the wreck.
- **Oloran:** The chaplain of the original garrison (10 years prior). His prayer to Procan caused the storm that sank the _Tammeraut_.
