# Inns/Taverns

**The Gilded Anvil**

- Location: The Anvilmount Ward, the dwarven district known for its skilled artisans and terraced homes.  
- Description: A traditional dwarven tavern built into the mountainside, with sturdy stone walls, a roaring fireplace, and long wooden tables. The Gilded Anvil is known for its strong ale, hearty stews, and lively atmosphere, often filled with the sounds of laughter, singing, and the clinking of tankards.
- Notable NPCs:
    - Barli Stonebrew (Dwarf): The gruff but friendly owner and brewer of the Gilded Anvil. His ale is legendary among the dwarves of [[Val Taruum]].
    - Astrid Truehammer (Dwarf): A renowned dwarven warrior and frequent patron of the Gilded Anvil. She is always ready for a brawl or a drinking contest.

**The Fallen Gear**

- Location: Gearsmith Gardens, the gnomish district known for its eccentric inventors and bustling workshops.  
- Description: A whimsical tavern built on a series of interconnected platforms and balconies, with quirky decorations and contraptions throughout. The Fallen Gear is known for its inventive cocktails, experimental dishes, and lively conversations about the latest inventions and discoveries.
- Notable NPCs:
    - Tinker Toddlecog (Gnome): The eccentric owner and inventor of the Fallen Gear. His latest creation is a self-filling tankard that never runs dry.
    - Professor Phileas Fogbottom (Gnome): A gnomish explorer and frequent patron of the Fallen Gear. He often shares tales of his daring adventures and discoveries.

# Merchant Mile Shops

- **The Apothecary:** A stall offering a variety of potions, elixirs, and herbal remedies, catering to both adventurers and everyday citizens.
- **The Enchanter's Enclave:** A section of the market where gnomish enchanters offer their services, imbuing items with magical properties or repairing damaged enchantments.
- **The Armorsmith's Alley:** A section of the market dedicated to dwarven armorsmiths, where you can find high-quality armor of all types, from sturdy leather jerkins to impressive full plate armor.
- **The Weaponsmith's Way:** A row of stalls run by dwarven weaponsmiths, offering a wide variety of weapons, from simple daggers and swords to impressive battleaxes and enchanted weapons with special properties.
- **The Dwarven Delights:** A bakery stall filled with delicious dwarven treats, including hearty breads, savory pies, and rich, spiced cakes.
