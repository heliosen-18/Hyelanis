### Population:

- Approximately 2400; mostly human, some elf and half-elf.

### Government:

- Sorrowclutch Refuge is governed by a consortium of wealthy merchants, whose bizarre laws and decrees are commonly ignored.

### Notable Places:

- The ruins of Ealdthryth Keep.

### A few NPCs:

- Alyn Charpyl: Female Human Thief, Good. Alyn has tangled grey hair and grey eyes, and a thin nose. She wears leather armor and wields a club and darts. Alyn is quick-witted but reckless.
- Gare: Male Human Professional, Neutral. Gare has messy silver hair and bright hazel eyes, and a sharp nose. He wears fine clothing and a lynx fur cape. Gare has an animal companion, a red fox named Sybel.
- Withe: Female Human Thief, Evil. Withe is fair in appearance, with black hair and grey eyes. She wears leather armor and wields a short sword. Withe lost her shadow to a hag.
- Ealdgyth: Female Human Craftsman, Good. Ealdgyth has messy brown hair and blue eyes. She wears modest garments and several small tools hang from her belt. Ealdgyth is valiant but sharp-tongued.
- Valdi: Male Dwarf Illusionist, Evil. Valdi is tall, with thick auburn hair and large hazel eyes. He wears fine clothing and wields a quarterstaff and dagger. Valdi seeks to become an instructor at the Athenaeum of Igagus.

## Lower Brightfane Village

### Notable Places:

- Gery's Masonry: The workshop of a male human stonemason named Gery, known for his weirdly specific knowledge of giants.
- The Jade Runestone: A broken menhir of green jade, engraved with fey runes. It is said that any child born within a league of the stone upon the night of the new moon will live a charmed life.

### A few NPCs:

- Helia: Female Human Wizard, Evil. Helia is exceptionally beautiful, with grey hair and blue eyes. She wears ruby robes and wields a dagger. Helia has an arcane familiar, a tiny fire elemental named Beorhtio.
- Hura: Female Dwarf Fighter, Neutral. Hura has golden hair and bright hazel eyes, and a tribal tattoo on her arm. She wears banded mail and wields a flail and long bow. Hura secretly serves Amon, an ancient goddess of darkness.
- Gery Pethey: Male Human Cleric, Evil. Gery has a long face, with messy grey hair and light brown eyes. He wears splint mail and wields a mace. Gery has an animal companion, a ginger ferret named Nichye.
- Khimal: Female Dwarf Soldier, Neutral. Khimal is short, with copper hair and green eyes. She wears studded leather and wields a glaive-guisarme. Khimal is searching for the lost empire of Dorothon.
