**[[Lady Ebonshade]], Mistress of Whispers:** A striking and mysterious figure, [[Lady Ebonshade]] is known for her ability to extract secrets from the most guarded minds. Her long, raven-black hair seems to meld with the shadows around her, and her deep, resonant voice carries a haunting allure. She is skilled in the art of manipulation, using her charms and wiles to gather information from both mortals and fey alike. [[Lady Ebonshade]] often weaves elaborate webs of deceit to achieve the Covenant's objectives, ensuring that the organization's actions remain shrouded in darkness.  

**Lord Umbra, Harbinger of Curses:** Tall and imposing, Lord Umbra exudes an aura of quiet power. His eyes gleam with an unsettling intensity, and his dark robes ripple like shadows in perpetual motion. Lord Umbra specializes in crafting and bestowing curses, harnessing the malevolent energies of the Feywild to bring about suffering and discord. His curses range from subtle manipulations to catastrophic afflictions, and he revels in watching the ruin his curses can unleash upon unsuspecting victims.  

**Vespera, Shadowbound Enchantress:** Vespera is a figure of eerie beauty, her form seemingly composed of shifting shadows and faint whispers. With a penchant for enchantments and illusions, she can make herself and others appear and disappear at will. Her soft voice holds a hypnotic quality, and she often uses her talents to lure inquisitive mortals into the Covenant's grasp. Vespera's loyalty to [[The Gaunt Man]] is unswerving, and her affinity for manipulation through illusion makes her a master of subterfuge.  

**Gloomstrider, Warden of the Veil:** Gloomstrider is a hulking figure, clad in armor woven from the very shadows he commands. His massive frame belies his agility and stealth, as he moves without a sound and melds seamlessly with the darkness. He serves as the protector of the Covenant's hidden sanctums and secret passages, ensuring that the organization's activities remain concealed from those who might oppose them. Gloomstrider's loyalty to the Covenant is unwavering, and he is fiercely protective of [[The Gaunt Man]]'s vision.  

**Silentweaver, Mistress of Dark Arts:** Silentweaver is a figure of ethereal grace, her movements accompanied by the faint rustling of unseen garments. A spellcaster of formidable skill, she delves deep into the forbidden realms of necromancy and shadow manipulation. Her spells are laced with whispers from beyond, and her magic has the power to bind spirits and manipulate the fabric of reality. Silentweaver's knowledge of dark arts makes her both a formidable ally and a dangerous foe.
