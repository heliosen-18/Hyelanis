**1. **Commander Seraphina Bowen**:**  
- Seraphina is a highly respected and battle-hardened leader of [[The Saffron Guard]]. She has earned her reputation through years of dedicated service and unwavering commitment to the protection of [[Bastion Reach]]. Seraphina is not only a skilled tactician but also a formidable warrior, known for her mastery of swordsmanship and arcane magic. She is often seen at the front lines, leading her troops with valor and determination.
 
**2. **Captain Lucius Bevan**:**  
- Lucius is an expert engineer and strategist within [[The Saffron Guard]]. His knowledge of Romanesque fortifications and siege warfare has been instrumental in maintaining and improving Demon Watch's defenses. He is known for his meticulous planning and the ingenious mechanical traps and deterrents he has designed to thwart potential intruders from The World Break.
 
**3. **Lorekeeper Elara Llewellyn**:**  
- Elara is a gifted scholar and mage, responsible for documenting the interactions between [[Bastion Reach]] and The World Break. Her expertise in arcane lore and her ability to communicate with beings from other realms make her a valuable asset to [[The Saffron Guard]]'s diplomatic efforts. She has a vast library of records detailing past encounters and negotiations.
 
**4. **Sentinel Bran d'Ifan**:**  
- Bran is a legendary dwarven warrior whose unwavering dedication to duty has made him a symbol of resilience within [[The Saffron Guard]]. He is a master of melee combat, wielding a massive warhammer with unmatched skill. Despite his gruff exterior, Bran has a heart of gold and is known for mentoring young recruits in the art of combat and discipline.
 
**5. **Emissary Amara Gwyn**:**  
- Amara is a gifted diplomat and mediator within [[The Saffron Guard]]. She possesses a natural affinity for negotiation and communication, making her the go-to choice for handling delicate situations with beings from The World Break. Her ability to find common ground and establish peaceful dialogues has prevented numerous potential conflicts and incursions.
 
**6. **Chaplain Alistair Rhys**:**  
- Alistair serves as the spiritual guide of [[The Saffron Guard]], offering solace and strength to its members. He is a devout cleric with a deep connection to the divine, and his healing magic has saved countless lives on the battlefield. Alistair's unwavering faith and moral compass inspire the Guard to stand firm in the face of darkness.
 
These prominent figures of [[The Saffron Guard]] represent a diverse range of skills and qualities, each contributing to the organization's effectiveness in defending Demon Watch and [[The Moss Wall]]. Together, they embody the values of courage, duty, and sacrifice that have defined [[The Saffron Guard]] throughout its storied history.
