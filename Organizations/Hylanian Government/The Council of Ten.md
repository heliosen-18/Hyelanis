- **The High Lord (The Emperor Jarin Sulla)**: The High Lord would be the leader of the council and would have overall responsibility for the running of the empire. They would be responsible for making decisions, resolving disputes, and ensuring that the council is working together effectively.
    - Leader of the council and overall ruler of the empire.
    - Responsible for decision-making, conflict resolution, and maintaining council harmony.
   

- **The 1st Lord of War (****Kaldar Ironheart)****:** The Lord of War would be responsible for the empire's military forces. They would be responsible for training, equipping, and deploying the military, as well as planning and executing military campaigns.
    - Oversees the empire's military forces.
    - Responsible for training, equipping, deploying, and strategizing military campaigns
        - _Race: Dwarven_
        - _Backstory: Kaldar Ironheart, a battle-hardened dwarf, rose through the ranks of the empire's military with unmatched tactical acumen and unwavering dedication. His skill in leading troops to victory and deep understanding of strategy earned him the position of 1st Lord of War. Kaldar's leadership inspires both fear and loyalty, making him a formidable force on the battlefield and a respected figure among the dwarven and non-dwarven military alike._
   

- **The 2nd Lord of the Land (Elara Kelmaris):** This lord would be in charge of the empire's land, including agriculture, mining, and forestry. They would be responsible for ensuring that the land is used efficiently and that the empire's resources are managed sustainably.
    - Manages the empire's land, including agriculture, mining, and forestry.
    - Ensures efficient land use and sustainable resource management.
        - _Race: Elven_
        - _Backstory: Elara Thornwood, an elven druid, possesses an unparalleled connection to nature. Having traveled through the empire's lands for centuries, she witnessed the delicate balance of ecosystems and the impact of resource utilization. Her commitment to sustainability and harmony with the land earned her the title of 2nd Lord of the Land. Elara strives to ensure that the empire's growth doesn't come at the cost of nature's well-being._
      
      
    
- **The 3rd Lord of Justice (Theron Dorsk)**: The Lord of Justice would be responsible for the empire's legal system. They would be responsible for ensuring that the law is applied fairly and consistently, as well as punishing those who break the law.
    - Oversees the empire's legal system.
    - Ensures fair and consistent application of the law, administers justice, and punishes lawbreakers.
        - _Race: Human_
        - _Backstory: Theron Brightblade, a skilled human paladin, has dedicated his life to upholding justice and righteousness. After years of service as a renowned knight, he assumed the role of 3rd Lord of Justice. Theron's unyielding pursuit of fairness and his reputation for incorruptibility make him the embodiment of the empire's legal system, earning respect from both commoners and nobles._

    - Manages the empire's finances.
    - Collects taxes, manages the budget, and maintains economic stability.
        - _Race: Halfling_

    - Leads the empire's trade efforts.
    - Negotiates trade agreements, promotes commerce, and ensures availability of goods and services.

They also would oversee the construction and maintenance of public infrastructure, such as roads, bridges, and public buildings, to improve the quality of life for the citizens.
    - Acts as a liaison between citizens and the imperial court.
    - Represents citizens' concerns, oversees public infrastructure projects, and improves quality of life.
        - _Race: Gnomish_
        - _Backstory: Lilia Hearthstone, a compassionate gnome engineer, has dedicated her life to improving the lives of the common folk. With innovative ideas and unending empathy, she was appointed as the 6th Lord of [[The People]]. Lilia's ingenious infrastructure projects have reshaped cities and bolstered quality of life. Her passion for the people drives her commitment to a harmonious society._
