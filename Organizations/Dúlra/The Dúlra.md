Keepers of the four elements. The High Druidic order that governs all organized Druidic groves/circles. The Starosti is the leading group of elders that leads the Dúlra. Each of the Dúlras have a single Starosta that is the leader of the their order. The Starosti's Meeting House, headquarters for the order, is in [[Meadowmist]] in [[Xabarin Wildlands]].
