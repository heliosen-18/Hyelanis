The Rift Reavers are a clandestine organization obsessed with the exploration and exploitation of planar rifts. They believe that by harnessing the power of these interdimensional gateways, they can unlock untold magical potential and reshape reality to their will. However, their reckless pursuit of power threatens to destabilize the delicate balance between [[The Planes]], potentially leading to catastrophic consequences.  

# **Backstory**  
The Rift Reavers' origins can be traced back to a group of renegade mages and scholars who delved into forbidden planar magic. They discovered ancient texts and rituals that hinted at the existence of powerful rifts connecting the Material Plane to other dimensions. Driven by curiosity and ambition, they formed a secret society dedicated to exploring and harnessing these rifts.  
Over time, the Rift Reavers grew in both knowledge and power, attracting individuals obsessed with the mysteries of the multiverse. They established hidden bases of operation near known rifts, conducting dangerous experiments and seeking to unlock the secrets of planar travel and manipulation.  

# **Description**  
The Rift Reavers are a diverse group, united by their fascination with planar rifts. Their ranks include mages, warlocks, artificers, and scholars, each with unique skills and motivations. Some seek knowledge and understanding, while others crave power and control. This diversity can lead to internal conflicts and shifting alliances within the organization.  

# **Key Figures**
- **The Rift Master:** A powerful mage and the enigmatic leader of the Rift Reavers. Their identity is shrouded in secrecy, known only to a select few within the organization. They are rumored to possess vast knowledge of planar magic and a dangerous obsession with merging [[The Planes]].
- **The Gatekeeper:** A skilled artificer responsible for creating and maintaining the Rift Reavers' equipment and technology. They are a master of enchanting and inscription, crafting devices that can detect, stabilize, and even manipulate planar rifts.
- **The Seer:** A prophetic mage who can glimpse into the chaotic flux of [[The Planes]], providing the Rift Reavers with visions of potential rifts and glimpses of the future. Their prophecies are often cryptic and unreliable, but they offer tantalizing hints of the power that awaits those who can harness the rifts.
- **The Harbinger:** A charismatic leader who serves as the public face of the Rift Reavers, recruiting new members and spreading their ideology. They are skilled at manipulating others, weaving tales of wonder and power to lure unsuspecting individuals into their ranks.

# **Methods**  
The Rift Reavers employ a variety of methods to achieve their goals:

- **Exploration:** They send expeditions into [[The Planes]], seeking to map out the network of rifts and discover new dimensions.
- **Experimentation:** They conduct dangerous experiments, attempting to stabilize and control planar rifts, often with unpredictable and devastating results.
- **Infiltration:** They infiltrate key institutions and organizations, seeking to gain access to resources, knowledge, and powerful artifacts that can aid in their research.
- **Manipulation:** They manipulate individuals and events, using their influence to further their agenda and eliminate those who oppose them.

# **Goals**  
The Rift Reavers' ultimate goal is to harness the power of planar rifts to reshape reality to their will. They believe that by merging [[The Planes]], they can unlock untold magical potential and usher in a new era of existence. However, their reckless pursuit of power could have catastrophic consequences, potentially destabilizing the fabric of reality and unleashing chaos upon the multiverse.
