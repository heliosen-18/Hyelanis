The Pack (Shifter) - Keep

Territory: Unicorn Lane, The Warrens, Centennial Park

Largest shifter society known possibly in the nation. By the latest official count, the Pack includes twelve hundred shapeshifters and is constantly growing. Consisting of 32 species divided into 7 Clans, with an organized military discipline, the members of the Pack value loyalty, obedience, restraint, and duty above all else.
