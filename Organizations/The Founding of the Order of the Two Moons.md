# The Haven of [[Lunaris]]
Inspired by their shared vision, Alistair and Selene established [[The Order of the Two Moons]] in a hidden valley called [[Lunaris]], named after the mystical event that brought them together. [[Lunaris]] was an ethereal haven, surrounded by dense forests, shimmering lakes, and a majestic waterfall cascading down from the mountains. The guild was built within an ancient citadel, its architecture reflecting a harmonious blend of elven elegance and human craftsmanship. From its humble beginnings, The Order grew in both size and reputation. Mages from far and wide sought to join its ranks, enticed by the promise of knowledge, camaraderie, and the opportunity to unlock the secrets of the moons' enchantments. The guild became a melting pot of magical traditions, where wizards, sorcerers, warlocks, and enchanters could share their expertise, learn from one another, and push the boundaries of arcane understanding. 

# Principles and the Pursuit of Knowledge
The Order's dedication to balance and harmony resonated throughout the guild's teachings. Mages were encouraged to explore various magical disciplines, embracing both the elemental forces of fire, water, earth, and air, as well as the more esoteric arts of illusion, divination, and necromancy. The guild's libraries housed a vast collection of ancient tomes, grimoires, and scrolls, carefully preserved by scribes and acolytes. Each moonlit night, members would gather under the celestial glow, conducting rituals and harnessing the mystic energies to advance their magical prowess.

# Role in [[O'Thalas]]'s Affairs and Challenges
Beyond their pursuit of knowledge, The Order of the Two Moons also played a vital role in [[O'Thalas]]'s affairs. Mages were dispatched as diplomats, healers, and protectors of the realm, using their abilities to maintain peace and balance. The guild maintained a neutral stance in conflicts, offering counsel and mediation when kingdoms clashed, and actively working to prevent the misuse of magic. However, The Order was not without its challenges. Rival factions and covetous sorcerers sought to exploit the guild's wealth of arcane knowledge for their own gain. In response, the mages of The Order stood as defenders, ensuring that magic remained a force of good in the world. They established a rigorous initiation process to ensure that only those who possessed a genuine desire for enlightenment and an unwavering commitment to the principles of balance were welcomed into their ranks.

# A Beacon of Light
Today, The Order of the Two Moons stands as a beacon of light and wisdom in a world often consumed by darkness. It continues to foster the growth of mages, nurturing their abilities and guiding them on the path of enlightenment. Under the benevolent guidance of its leadership, the guild strives to keep the arcane arts in balance, preserve ancient knowledge, and ensure that the Two Moons' blessings shine upon [[O'Thalas]] for generations to come.
