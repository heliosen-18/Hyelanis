Goddess of Snow
 
Khione has a mane of lush black hair, coffee-brown eyes, and unnaturally pale skin that was the color of snow. Khione was also said to be the fairest and most beautiful woman in all the land. She wore a white dress, and sometimes had a silver crown atop her hair.
 
As the Goddess of Snow, Khione has divine authority and absolute control over ice, snow, and cold.
